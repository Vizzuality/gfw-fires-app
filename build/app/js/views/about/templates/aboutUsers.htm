Users

GFW-Commodities aims to radically improve the availability, quality, and accessibility of forest data for a diverse range of business stakeholders. For example:

  1. Commodity buyers can use the forest change and concession data on GFW-Commodities to inform purchasing decisions based on supplier practices related to deforestation.
  2. Producers can use the palm oil suitability mapping tool to inform decisions on where and how to expand production.
  3. Sustainability officers can use the forest change and concession data to assess compliance with company sourcing policies and commitments to zero deforestation.
  4. Forest-product companies can use forest change and concession data to illustrate good practices and compliance to standards and certifications and communicate with customers.
  5. Palm oil companies can use the Roundtable on Sustainable Palm Oil (RSPO) analysis tool to comply with RSPO requirements.
  6. NGOs can identify companies advancing deforestation and bolster their investigations, advocacy, and campaigns as well as those who are operating responsibly.
  7. Media can gather evidence for reporting on corporate commitments to sustainable production.
  8. Governments can use GFW-Commodities to detect illegal forest clearing and target forest law enforcement efforts to those specific companies.
  9. Researchers can analyze trends in the contribution to deforestation from commodity production.
